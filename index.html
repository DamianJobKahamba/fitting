How to Take Photos for Accurate Measurements

Front Photo

  • Stand straight with feet shoulder-width apart
  • Arms slightly away from body
  • Wear form-fitting clothes
  • Ensure full body is visible

Side Photo

  • Stand with your side to the camera
  • Keep your back straight
  • Arms at your sides
  • Ensure full body is visible

For best results

Take photos against a plain background with good lighting. Have someone else take the photo or use a timer.
